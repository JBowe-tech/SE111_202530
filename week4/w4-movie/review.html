Review of Fairy Tail: Dragon Cry

Jayda Bowe April 14th, 2025 ★★★★★

Fairy Tail: Dragon Cry is one of my favorite movies and Fairy Tail the anime is one of my favortie anime series, it is the first anime I finished on my own and was recommended to me by my cousin. Having said that I hold this movie close to my heart as it captures emotional moments of my favorite characters as well as their growth through the perils they faced. The fights between the main characters and villians in my opinion are very well animated and I enjoued watching them grow as they fought new stronger oponents. The movie isn't a completely joyful ride as it does contain violent dipictions. As the movie is also about an artifact created by pain and suffering the ending of the movie tha shows the release of the all the sorrow and anger contained in the object is refreshing and touching. Personally I loved watching it, when I was younder and watched it for the first time it really touvhed me as I watched Natsu cry for the fallen dragons that he felt so connected to although he never met any of them.
